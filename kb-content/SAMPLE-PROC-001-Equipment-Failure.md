# SAMPLE-PROC-001: Equipment Failure Procedure

> Sample document — replace with your own procedures.

## Scope
Applies when a piece of equipment at the worksite stops functioning or shows a fault (point-of-sale terminal, refrigeration unit, printer, or any operational device).

## Urgency Levels
- **High** (resolve within 1 hour): payment terminal down, perishable refrigeration off, security system offline
- **Medium** (resolve within 4 hours): printer not working, secondary appliance fault
- **Low** (resolve within 24 hours): minor lighting issue, cosmetic damage

## Step-by-Step
1. **Basic diagnostics** — confirm the device is powered on, check cable connections, and restart it (hold the power button for 10 seconds).
2. **If diagnostics don't resolve it** — log a maintenance report in the system with the device name, the problem, and the steps already taken. The system assigns a ticket number and notifies technical support.
3. **Temporary measures** — if a payment terminal is down, switch to a backup terminal or contact a supervisor to authorize manual sales. If refrigeration is off, do not open the doors; after 2 hours move perishables and record the loss.
4. **Follow-up** — note the ticket number. If it is high urgency and there is no response within 30 minutes, escalate to a supervisor.

## Support Contacts
- Technical support: ext. 5001
- General support: ext. 5000

## Important Notes
- Never attempt to repair electrical equipment yourself.
- If there is a burning smell or smoke, disconnect immediately and evacuate if necessary.
- Document the equipment state with photos for the report.
