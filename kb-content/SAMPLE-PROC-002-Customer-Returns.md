# SAMPLE-PROC-002: Customer Returns Procedure

> Sample document — replace with your own procedures.

## Scope
Applies when a customer requests a return or refund for a purchased product.

## General Policy
- Returns accepted within **7 calendar days** of purchase.
- Original **receipt required** (except special cases below).
- Product must be in its **original packaging** and unused (unless defective).
- Refunds issued as cash or store credit.

## Step-by-Step
1. **Verify the receipt** — confirm the purchase date is within the window and the product appears on the receipt.
2. **Inspect the product** — check it is in acceptable condition. Opened or used items are only accepted if defective.
3. **Return without receipt** — look up the transaction in the system by approximate date, payment method, or amount. If found, proceed; if not, the return cannot be processed.
4. **Process in the system** — open the returns menu, scan the receipt or enter the transaction number, select the product and reason, confirm the amount, and issue the refund.

## Items Not Eligible
- Activated gift cards or phone top-ups
- Opened personal-care items
- Perishable goods past their date

## Important Notes
- If the customer becomes upset, stay calm and offer to escalate to a supervisor.
- Log all returns in the daily record.
- If you suspect fraud (frequent returns from the same customer), notify a supervisor.
