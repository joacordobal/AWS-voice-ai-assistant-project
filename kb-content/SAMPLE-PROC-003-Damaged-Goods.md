# SAMPLE-PROC-003: Damaged or Expired Goods Procedure

> Sample document — replace with your own procedures.

## Scope
Applies when a worker finds damaged, broken, expired, or spoiled products on the shelf, in storage, or when receiving merchandise.

## Step-by-Step
1. **Identify** — visually check the product state (dented, broken, spilled, past date). Note the product name, barcode, quantity affected, and location.
2. **Remove immediately** — pull the product from the sales area and place it in the designated write-off area. Never sell damaged or expired products.
3. **Register in the system** — log a write-off entry: scan the barcode, select the reason (damaged or expired), enter the quantity, and confirm.
4. **Photo evidence** — take a clear photo of the damage. For expired products, photograph the expiry date.
5. **Final disposition** — separate expired products for supplier pickup or disposal per policy. For high-value items, always notify a supervisor.

## Important Notes
- Write-offs must be recorded the same day they are found.
- If the write-off quantity exceeds a defined threshold, notify a supervisor.
- For high-value categories, always involve a supervisor.
